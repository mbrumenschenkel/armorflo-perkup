Program Overview

The ArmorFlo Perk Up Program offers cash-back rebates to eligible consumers who purchase qualifying ArmorFlo products from participating automotive service providers and submit a valid receipt for verification within the promotion window.

Rebates are issued in the form of prepaid reward cards and are subject to verification, eligibility requirements, and availability as described in these Terms.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a rebate, you must:

  • Be a legal resident of the United States, 18 years of age or older.
  • Purchase one or more qualifying ArmorFlo products during the valid promotion period.
  • Submit a legible receipt showing the qualifying product(s), purchase date, and retailer within the submission window.
  • Provide a valid email address and/or phone number for delivery of your reward.

Employees of Cadence Petroleum Group, its affiliates, distributors, and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate.

Only products purchased during the valid promotion window qualify. The current promotion window is displayed on the submission page. Receipts dated outside this window will not be approved.

AI determinations are made automatically. If you believe your submission was incorrectly denied, you may contact support at https://www.cadenceperkup.com/support for manual review within 30 days of your submission.
